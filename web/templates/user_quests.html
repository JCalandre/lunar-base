Quest Editor

Tick quests and press CLEAR CHOSEN. Each clear replays lunar-tear's real finish — it grants the first-clear, mission and drop rewards, marks the quest cleared, unlocks the next quest/difficulty, and records its side-story scenario, exactly like playing it. Already-cleared quests are skipped. A backup is taken automatically before every change.

Already-cleared quests can be re-run for farming: tick them and press REDO ×N. Each redo replays the finish N times, granting only the repeatable drop/mission rewards — the one-time first-clear bonus is never re-awarded.

Main-story quests should be cleared in order. Skipping a quest's predecessors can leave your story progress inconsistent (the next chapter may not open correctly). Events are self-contained — tick the lower difficulty before the higher one so the unlock cascades.
